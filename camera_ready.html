Camera-Ready Instructions

For camera-ready papers for the EMDL'26 proceedings, please do the following:

  • Fill the ACM rights management form.
  • Upload the camera-ready version.
  • Upload your video presentation.
Complete rights management form
  • Before submitting your camera-ready versions, you need to complete the rights management form. You will be notified by email once these forms are available and ready to sign.
Format paper
  • Full papers should be no more than 6 pages, including references. Work-in-Progress papers should be no more than 2 pages, including references.
  • Note that the camera-ready version should use the latest ACM template and can use either 10pt or 9pt font size.
  • After completing the rights management form, you will be emailed the correct rights management text and bibliographic strip to place within your paper. It is essential that you add the text into your paper.
Submit camera-ready version
  • Go to the EMDL 2026 submission site and edit your paper: https://emdl26.hotcrp.com/

Any questions regarding submission issues should be directed to Young D. Kwon (youngkwon311@gmail.com) or Stelios Venieris (s.venieris@samsung.com).
